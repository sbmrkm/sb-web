Who is H?

Henri Cartier-Bresson

(French: [kaʁtje bʁɛsɔ̃]; 22 August 1908 – 3 August 2004) was a French humanist photographer considered a master of candid photography

Henri Cartier-Bresson was born in Chanteloup-en-Brie, Seine-et-Marne, France.

His father was a wealthy textile manufacturer, whose Cartier-Bresson thread was a staple of French sewing kits. His mother's family were cotton merchants and landowners from Normandy, where Henri spent part of his childhood. His mother was descended from Charlotte Corday
